My Anime List

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation that premiered in 1999. It is based on the manga series of the same name written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. The series follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after eating a Devil Fruit, and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as they search for the world’s greatest treasure known as the “One Piece” to become the next Pirate King

My views

Absolute peak anime. There is a reason this is one of big 3. Some people might feel this is long and dont start it but when u watch it u feel like this is still short.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a popular Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Hajime Isayama. The anime is set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to the sudden appearance of giant humanoid creatures known as Titans, who devour humans seemingly without reason.

My views

An epic anime that shows friendship and scrifice. A great story with a little bit love story. A must watch anime.

Bleach

Absolutely! Bleach is a popular action-adventure anime series based on the manga of the same name by Tite Kubo. The story follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a hot-headed high school student who can see spirits. The anime was produced by Studio Pierrot and directed by Noriyuki Abe, with music composed by Shirō Sagisu.

My views

Bleach is a very good anime. It has many fillers that could be skipped. But there is a reason it is in big 3. The suspence,the entrences are just epic.It has some of the top notch fights and have too good BGM's. A must watch too.

Mashle Magic and muscles

Mashle: Magic and Muscles is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Kōmoto. It was serialized in Shueisha’s shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from January 2020 to July 2023, with its chapters collected in 18 tankōbon volumes.

My views

This was a very fun anime to watch.This was probably inspired by Harry Potter. This was funny and shows friendship. This anime is on a boy who is misfit for world. The world is where everyone has magic but our protagnist have only muscles(physical power).

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a South Korean anime television series based on the web novel of the same name written by Chugong. The anime is produced by A-1 Pictures and directed by Shunsuke Nakashige, with Noboru Kimura writing the scripts, Tomoko Sudo designing the characters, and Hiroyuki Sawano composing the music.

My views

Anime starts with a weak boy. Who is known as weakest hunter of all time. He goes to a near death experience. Then he gets starting leveling up(powering up). A great journey of reaching top. Eagerly waiting for S2.

Ninja Kamui

Ninja Kamui is a Japanese-American anime series directed by Sunghoo Park and produced by Adult Swim, E&H Production, and Sola Entertainment. The series premiered on Adult Swim's Toonami programming block on February 10, 2024.

My views

This anime is probably inspired by John Wick. First episode of this anime was one of the best first episode I watched of any anime. It had some of most epic fights I watched. Fights animation,powers and fighting style were some of the best.

Misfit of Demon king Academy

The Misfit of Demon King Academy is a fantasy anime series based on the light novel series of the same title written by Shu and illustrated by Yoshinori Shizuma. The anime follows the story of Anos Voldigoad, a 2000-year-old legendary Demon King who reincarnates into the modern world and enrolls in the Demon King Academy, a prestigious school for magic users.

My views

The anime is about an over power protagonist who is reincarnation of demon king himself.He comes back and finishes a fight between demon and heros. An over power protagnist animes are kind of animes I like. He makes some friends along the way. This is also a must watch anime.

Tokyo Revengers

Tokyo Revengers is a Japanese anime television series based on the manga series of the same name by Ken Wakui. The series follows the story of Takemichi Hanagaki, a 26-year-old man who is struggling to find purpose in his life. After being informed of the death of his childhood friends, Takemichi is transported back in time to his middle school days, where he decides to take a different path to prevent their tragic fate.

My views

This anime is of a weak boy. Who is going back and forth into past and future to save his friends and future. In season 1 and 2 you might feel annoyed by how weak MC is but side characters make up for it. S3 is prtty good. Suggested to watch.
